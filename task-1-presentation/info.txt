I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva, which is a simple and user-friendly design tool. In the infographic, I covered important topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online presence, and proper email etiquette. I selected these topics because they are very important for students in today’s digital world and can help in both academics and future careers.

The design is kept simple and easy to understand by using short points and relevant icons. It focuses on practical tips like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and maintaining a clean online profile.

While working on this task, I found it interesting to explore different layouts and design options in Canva. However, arranging all the content neatly in one page was a bit challenging. Overall, this activity helped me understand how important digital literacy is and how it can be shared effectively through visuals.
